DEAR NOVA....

I didn’t realize when it began,
only that your presence slowly
became something I looked forward to
without needing a reason.

You appear in my thoughts softly,
not loud or demanding,
just enough to make my heart
pause and smile.

There is comfort in the way you exist,
in your laughter, in your silence,
as if the world feels lighter
when you are near.

I admire you from a quiet distance,
holding feelings I don’t rush,
letting them grow naturally,
untouched by fear.

This isn’t a love that asks for much,
only the chance to feel,
to appreciate someone
as they truly are.

Maybe this feeling will fade,
or maybe it will bloom into more,
but right now it’s beautiful
simply because it exists.

So I keep this crush gentle and true,
a small secret my heart holds,
hoping one day you’ll know
what you already mean to me.
